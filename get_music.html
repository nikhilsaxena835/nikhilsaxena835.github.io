What is this tool?

The Problem It Solves

Modern music streaming platforms are built around algorithms designed to keep you listening to what you already like. While convenient, this creates an "echo chamber" where true discovery becomes incredibly difficult.

Album Discovery was built to solve this by completely removing recommendation algorithms, taste profiling, and personal biases. Instead, it relies on pure geographical curation and randomness. A user might discover Japanese City Pop one day, Indian Carnatic the next, and Brazilian Jazz after that. It surfaces culturally diverse and historically important albums from around the globe, ensuring that your music journey is serendipitous rather than mathematically predicted.

Want to know how it's built?

The curation is via Wikidata SPARQL, Last.fm, and MusicBrainz. The app itself is fully serverless, running on Cloudflare Pages, KV Cache, and D1 (SQLite).

How to use it

You need to choose a unique username for first time. This username will be stored in a database alongwith your liked, disliked and skipped albums. That's it. Everytime you visit, just enter your username and your state will be fetched. No cookies used here.

🌅 Daily Album: A single, globally curated pick every day. Everyone around the world sees the same album.

🔍 Filtered Discovery: Use dropdown menus to filter albums by specific Countries, Genres, and Decades.

🌍 World Tour: Explores music from a completely random country you haven't recently visited to fill out your "passport".

💎 Hidden Gem: Surfaces critically acclaimed or historically important albums that have low global playcounts.

Interacting

When an album is presented, you can choose to:

  • 👍 Like: Add it to your Liked history.
  • 👎 Dislike: Ensure you won't see it again.
  • ⏭️ Skip: Not interested right now.
